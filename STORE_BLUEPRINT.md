## Legal Copy Guidance

- Do not use investment language.
- Do not promise profit, resale value, appreciation, ROI, or financial return.
- Do not use staking rewards language unless reviewed.
- Publish odds for blind boxes and booster packs where required.
- Use collectible, game utility, access, fan experience, and merch language.
- Clearly distinguish physical products from digital unlocks.
- Clearly distinguish future roadmap items from live functionality.
