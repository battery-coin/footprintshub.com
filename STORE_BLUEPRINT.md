# Store Blueprint

## Purpose

Footprints Hub Store is the standalone commerce engine for Footprints Hub, Matrix Decoded, Hero Studio creator commerce, collectibles, merch, blind boxes, booster packs, founder bundles, digital unlocks, and future Battery Coin utility checkout.

## Initial Catalog Areas

- Footprints music merchandise
- Matrix Decoded trading cards
- Booster packs
- Blind boxes
- Team Tardee plushes
- Satoshi Nakamoto collectibles
- Hero Studio creator drops
- Fan club merchandise
- Supporter bundles
- Limited edition collectibles
- Future digital twin registration products
- Future token-gated utility products

## Initial Category Plan

- Music Merch
- Trading Cards

- Blind Boxes
- Plushes
- Collectibles
- Creator Drops
- Fan Club
- Founder Bundles
- Digital Unlocks

## Ownership Boundary

Magento owns products, carts, checkout, customers, orders, invoices, shipments, refunds, catalog pricing, and commerce tax/shipping/payment configuration.

Hero Studio owns fan, audition, campaign, creator, social, and engagement workflows.
