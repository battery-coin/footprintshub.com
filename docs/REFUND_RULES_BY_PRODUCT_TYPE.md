# Refund Rules By Product Type

- Physical: standard return/refund policy.
- Print-on-demand: manual review after production starts.
- Digital downloads: manual review after access or download; access may be revoked.
- Services: depends on brief, scheduling, work performed, and delivered status.
- Subscriptions: cancel future billing; current-period refund depends on policy.
- NFT-linked products: manual review; onchain actions may be irreversible.
- Bundles: evaluate each component by product type.

Affiliate commissions should reverse only after a refund is confirmed.
