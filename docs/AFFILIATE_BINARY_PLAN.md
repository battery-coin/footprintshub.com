# Affiliate Binary Plan

Date: 2026-05-10

## Purpose

The Binary structure lets the owner configure a left/right ambassador network view and weaker-leg or pair-matching style settings.

## Owner Editable Settings

- Left team label
- Right team label
- Payout basis
- Pair ratio
- Pair commission percentage
- Pair fixed amount placeholder
- Spillover mode
- Carry-forward volume
- Flush-after-payout setting
- Max pairs per period placeholder
- Per-order and monthly caps through plan levels

## MVP Status

Binary is configurable, persisted, previewable, and calculator-ready. The real payout engine remains scaffolded until placement, volume carry-forward, and period closeout are hardened with tests.

## Compliance Guardrail

Binary commissions must be based only on qualified purchase volume. No commission is paid solely for recruiting or enrolling another affiliate.
