# Future Wallet Payment Providers

## P0

- Coinbase Business Checkouts API: hosted checkout, provider monitoring, webhook-based status.

## P1

- Coinbase Wallet and WalletConnect identity connection.
- Signed wallet ownership verification.

## P2/P3

- Direct onchain stablecoin payment.
- WalletConnect payment flows if provider support and reconciliation are production ready.
- Battery Coin utility checkout after legal, accounting, provider, and security review.

## Why Hosted Checkout First

Hosted provider checkout reduces reconciliation risk, keeps payment status server-side, supports webhook workflows, and avoids marking orders paid from client wallet actions.
