## Affiliate Workflow

1. Apply to become a FootprintsHub affiliate.
2. Accept affiliate terms and disclosure rules.
3. Wait for admin approval.
4. Share referral links such as `https://footprintshub.com/?ref=CODE`.
5. Use product links for specific products.
6. Track clicks, orders, pending commissions, approved commissions, payouts, and wallet activity.
7. Request a payout once the available balance meets the shop minimum.

## Important Rules

- Commissions are paid only on qualified purchases.
- No guaranteed earnings are promised.
- Self-referrals and suspicious activity can be rejected.
- Refunds and chargebacks can reverse commissions.
- Affiliates must disclose commission relationships in public posts and campaigns.
