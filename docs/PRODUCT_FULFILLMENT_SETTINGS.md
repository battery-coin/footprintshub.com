# Product Fulfillment Settings

Date: 2026-05-10

## Fulfillment Types

- Manual
- Printful
- Digital
- Internal
- Mixed

## Printful

The editor includes product and sync product mapping fields, plus variant-level Printful IDs. Paid-order Printful submission should still happen only after verified payment.

## Digital Goods

Digital products can disable shipping and include digital unlock settings.

## Blind Box and Booster Pack

The editor includes blind box and booster pack toggles, plus an odds disclosure URL field. Public odds disclosures must be configured before selling randomized products.
