# Mixed Payment Affiliate Rules

Affiliate commissions are calculated only after full qualified payment completion.

Rules:

- No commission after only fiat partial payment.
- No commission after only token partial payment.
- No commission if token payment fails or expires.
- MVP commission value remains USD cents after full payment.
- Token-only and mixed orders remain eligible only after full payment completion.

Commissions are paid only on qualified purchases and never for recruitment alone.
