# Printful Legal Copy

Suggested customer-facing copy:

> Some products are produced and fulfilled by third-party print-on-demand fulfillment partners after your order is paid. Production and shipping times may vary by product and destination.

Policies should cover:

- Production time is separate from shipping time.
- Address accuracy is the customer responsibility before fulfillment begins.
- Print-on-demand/custom items may have different return rules.
- Damaged or defective products should be reported with photos and order details.
- International customs, duties, or import taxes may apply where relevant.

Do not promise guaranteed delivery dates unless the shipping provider and policy explicitly support them.
