# Ad Legal Copy

Added legal routes:

- `/legal/advertising-terms`
- `/legal/sponsorship-terms`
- `/legal/ad-content-policy`
- `/legal/ad-refunds`

Ad copy avoids performance guarantees unless a package explicitly states a deliverable.

Prohibited content includes illegal products, misleading claims, harassment, hate, explicit adult content, unreviewed weapons/drug claims, false financial claims, and unsubstantiated medical claims.
