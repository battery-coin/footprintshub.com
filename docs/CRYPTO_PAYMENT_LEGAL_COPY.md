# Crypto Payment Legal Copy

Customer-facing copy should say:

- Crypto payments are processed by a third-party payment provider.
- Payment confirmation may take time.
- Orders are not confirmed until provider payment status is verified.
- Supported assets and networks are determined by the provider.
- Customers are responsible for sending payment through the correct provider flow.
- Refunds may depend on provider capability, network rules, and store policy.
- Blockchain transactions may be irreversible.
- Battery Coin or token checkout is a utility/payment feature only.

Avoid:

- investment
- profit
- ROI
- guaranteed gains
- passive income
- yield
- staking reward
- price appreciation
- resale guarantee
