# Affiliate Terms Copy

## Core Program Statement

The FootprintsHub affiliate and ambassador program pays commissions only on qualified purchases of real products, memberships, campaign merchandise, digital goods, creator shop purchases, supporter bundles, approved subscriptions, or approved commercial transactions.

No commission is paid merely for recruiting or enrolling another affiliate.

## Required Disclaimers

- No guaranteed earnings.
- No employment relationship.
- No investment relationship.
- No passive income promise.
- No payment for recruitment alone.
- Refunds and chargebacks can reverse commissions.
- Payouts require approval and may be held.
- Affiliates are responsible for taxes and legal marketing disclosures.
- Program rules, rates, eligibility, and payout policies may change.
- Fraud, self-referrals, misleading claims, or abuse may lead to rejection or suspension.

## Disclosure Reminder

Affiliates should disclose: "I may earn a commission from qualified purchases made through my link."
