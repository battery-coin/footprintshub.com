# Affiliate Business Models

FootprintsHub supports shop-specific affiliate and ambassador plans. These are purchase-commission models only. No commission is paid merely for recruiting or enrolling another affiliate.

## Supported Models

1. Flat Affiliate Commission: one direct rate for the referring affiliate.
2. Product-Specific Commission: rates override by product.
3. Category / Collection Commission: rates override by catalog grouping.
4. Campaign Commission: rates override for campaign products and dates.
5. Creator Shop Commission: each creator shop can have its own default plan.
6. Affiliate-Specific Commission: selected ambassadors can receive custom rates.
7. Rank-Based Commission: rank can unlock rate bonuses or paid depth.
8. Performance-Tier Commission: monthly qualified sales or orders can adjust rate/depth.
9. 7-Level Ambassador Commission: direct and ancestor commissions up to level 7.
10. Lifetime Customer Attribution: future purchases can credit the assigned affiliate when enabled.
11. Coupon Attribution: affiliate coupon can override link attribution when configured.
12. Store Credit Commission: commissions can be issued as store credit instead of cash.
13. Hybrid Plan: combines direct, product, rank, tier, coupon, lifetime, and 7-level rules.

## Safe Terminology

Use affiliate, ambassador, referral partner, referral network, qualified purchase commission, pending commission, approved commission, paid commission, wallet balance, payout request, and store credit.

Avoid customer-facing use of MLM, downline, passive income, recruiting income, pyramid, investment, ROI, financial freedom, yield, or guaranteed earnings.

## Reference
