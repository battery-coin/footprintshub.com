# Multi-Level Commission Rules

FootprintsHub supports up to seven configurable ancestor levels for qualified purchase commissions.

## Terminology

- Level 0: direct affiliate who referred the buyer
- Level 1: parent ambassador of the direct affiliate
- Level 2: grandparent ambassador
- Level 3: third ancestor
- Level 4: fourth ancestor
- Level 5: fifth ancestor
- Level 6: sixth ancestor
- Level 7: seventh ancestor

Avoid `downline`, `recruiting income`, `passive income`, `business opportunity`, `financial freedom`, and similar earnings-oriented language.

## Default Seed Settings

- Direct affiliate: 10% of qualified product subtotal
- Level 1: 2% of qualified order subtotal
- Level 2: 1%
- Level 3: 0.75%
- Level 4: 0.50%
- Level 5: 0.25%
- Level 6: 0.25%
- Level 7: 0.25%
- Default max commission pool: 20% of qualified product subtotal/order subtotal

These defaults are conservative placeholders. Shop owners should confirm margins and legal review before launch.

## Safety Rules

- No commission is paid merely for recruiting or enrolling another affiliate.
- All commissions start as pending.
- Own-referrals are blocked by default.
- Refunds and chargebacks reverse pending or approved commissions.
- Parent chains cannot be circular.
- An affiliate cannot be their own parent.
- Parent affiliates must belong to the same shop.
- Ancestor commissions stop at the configured active depth.
- Total commissions cannot exceed the configured order pool cap.
- Monthly and per-order caps can be configured per level.

## Future Structures

Only unilevel parent chains are implemented for MVP. Binary and forced-matrix layouts are documented as possible future structures, but should not be enabled without legal and compliance review.
