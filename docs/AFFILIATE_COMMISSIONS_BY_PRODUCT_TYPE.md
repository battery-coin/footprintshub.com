# Affiliate Commissions By Product Type

Affiliate commissions are created only after qualified purchase completion.

Suggested eligibility:

- Physical: enabled by default.
- Digital download: configurable.
- Service: configurable.
- Subscription: configurable for first payment, recurring payments, or both.
- NFT-linked products: disabled by default unless reviewed.
- Membership: configurable.
- Bundle: based on bundle subtotal or child item rules.

No commission is paid for recruitment alone.
