# Affiliate Performance Tiers

Performance tiers can change direct commission or max paid levels based on approved metrics.

## Example Monthly Sales Bands

- Starter: $0-$999 monthly qualified sales, 8% direct, level 0.
- Builder: $1,000-$4,999 monthly qualified sales, 10% direct, levels 0-3.
- Leader: $5,000+ monthly qualified sales, 12% direct, levels 0-7.

## Metrics

- monthly_sales
- monthly_orders
- lifetime_sales
- approved_commissions

## Safety Rule

Performance tiers must be based on real qualified purchases or approved commissions, not recruitment alone.
