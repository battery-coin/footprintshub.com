# Refund Legal Copy

Suggested customer-facing language:

## Refunds

Refund eligibility depends on the product type, order status, fulfillment status, and applicable policy. Refunds may be full or partial.

## Print-On-Demand

Some products are produced by third-party print-on-demand fulfillment partners after payment. Once production has started, cancellation or refund eligibility may require manual review.

## Digital Goods

Digital downloads and access products may be reviewed differently after access has been delivered or files have been downloaded. Unauthorized redistribution is not permitted.

## Services

Service refunds depend on scheduling, work performed, deliverables completed, and cancellation timing.

## Subscriptions

Subscription cancellation stops future billing according to the subscription terms. Current-period refunds depend on policy and usage.

## NFT-Linked Collectibles

NFT-linked products are digital collectibles, certificates, access credentials, or provenance records. Blockchain transactions may be irreversible. No investment value, resale outcome, profit, or appreciation is promised.

## Timing

Payment provider processing times may vary. FootprintsHub should not promise guaranteed refund arrival dates unless operations can support that promise.
