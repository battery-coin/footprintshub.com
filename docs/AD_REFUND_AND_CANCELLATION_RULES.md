# Ad Refund And Cancellation Rules

Suggested refund rules:

- Not reviewed: full refund may be allowed.
- Rejected creative: refund or request new creative.
- Approved but not live: admin decision.
- Live and partially delivered: partial refund or store credit.
- Completed: generally no refund unless policy exception.
- Recurring ad subscriptions: cancel future billing; current period handled by policy.

Affiliate commissions on ad purchases should reverse after refund or chargeback.
